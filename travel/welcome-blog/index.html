Welcome to my Travel Blog!

Just another travel blog, read and find it out yourself.

January 29, 2019 Jatin Dogra

2 minute read

Wondering, another travel blog, just another person writing his travel experiences.

Meh! Pass or not to pass?

Decide it yourself :)

Hi there,

Yesterday was the beginning & tomorrow there will be an end though somewhere in the middle we have to focus on things we need to do. Somewhere around August 2018 with a broken clavicle and a month of bed rest, was when I decided to start blogging and it took me around 6 months to actually start a travel blog. Was I lazy, probably yes but again I wanted to completely sure if I am going to do this, I need to find a work-life balance and more importantly stick to it. Have I found the balance? I don’t think so, but I think, I have found the will to stick to blogging. Nothing gives me more pleasure than travelling, exploring places, talking to people, learning from them, and I believe that is enough for me to stick to the purpose of starting a travel blog and penning down my experiences to share with you all.

While travelling gives me immense pleasure, but what inspires me to travel is a will inside me to touch every corner in the world. I believe that travelling to places you get to learn about their cultures, lifestyles. You start to see things from a different perspective and then you learn and grow. Plus, I am not a full-time traveller and don’t plan to do it in the near future. So I wish to inspire those who seek to travel while working full-time.

I hope this blog will treat as a medium to advocate my thoughts towards the notion of travelling and learn likewise from my fellow readers. There are some ground rules that I have set and one of them is at least 3 blog posts every month and an aim to grow the number with time. There are many ideas and thoughts in my mind to fulfil using this platform, but let’s take one step at a time. And I will reveal other things when the time is right.

Also, I would like to share that travel stories from Kerala is in pipeline for the upcoming month and I hope you will like it. That’s all for getting started. In addition, do follow my instagram for more updates on where I am travelling or planning to travel next.

PS: Your feedback is welcome and appreciated!

Peace & Love,

Jatin Dogra
