About My Quests

HI THERE, MY NAME IS JATIN DOGRA AND I AM CHASING MY QUESTS.

An amateur photographer, who loves to travel and explore new passages. So I started a new travel blog and here we are. Welcome to my blog!

I am here to share my experiences from my quests of travel. The idea behind the blog is to help fellow travellers in any way I can, let it be giving them a destination, a weekend escape or any other tips related to travelling. I hope this blog will treat as a medium to advocate my thoughts towards the notion of travelling.

Working full-time has never been a problem to indulge in my dreams to travel and explore. With the passion of travelling to almost every corner of the world, I wish to inspire those who aspire to do the same while working full-time.

Feel free to drop me a message anytime. It won’t take more than a minute :)

Thanks for reading!

Jatin Dogra
